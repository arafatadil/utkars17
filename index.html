ABOUT US

Utkarsh,the largest Hindi cultural fest of North India is one with a motive. Supported by MHRD and organised by ANAMIKA HINDI SOCIETY, the festival was started to promote the importance of Hindi language and with 11 extravagant celebrations,UTKARSH turns 12 this year. Every year the fest has grown and nurtured from plethora of memories, countless dreams and millions of screams. So be a part of something bigger this year, treasure and cherish the aura of joyous moments and witness the colorful blend of culture, drama, music, dance and passion.

Shan-A-Utkarsh

With a touch of glamour and passion, lads and ladies from all the colleges compete to show their talents for the prestigious title of “Shan-A-Utkarsh”.

Description :
First round : This is a written round in which the personality of the participants is tested.
Second round: This is a talent round in which participants are selected on the basis of their talent.
Final round : This round consists of some more sub rounds like fashion parade, audio visual round, performance round and questioning round.

Nukkad

It is a form of theatrical performance and presentation in outdoor public spaces without a specific paying audience. The logistics of doing Nukkad Natak necessitate simple costumes and props, with actors depending on their natural vocal and physical ability.

Description :
Time Limit: 25 minutes

Natya Manch

It is a form of theatrical performance and presentation on stage.

Description :
Time Limit: 25 minutes

Ekal Abhinay

It is an act of a single actor on the stage. Acting of the play on the stage consists of more than one character and is based on 'dialogues' i.e conversation among characters. All the ingredients of a stage play - light, costume, effect music etc can be can be used. Description :
Time Limit: 25 minutes

Raag(solo)

It is a musical event including all styles of music.

Description :
It is a solo performance.
Time Limit: 25 minutes
Participants are allowed to use any kind of musical instrument, karaoke for performance.

Raag(duet)

It is a musical duet performance. Any movie song in Indian language is allowed.

Description :
Time Limit: 25 minutes
2 participants form a team (Male-Male, Female-Female, & Male-Female).
Participants can take help of musical instrument accompanists or karaoke for performance.

Instrumental Solo

It is an event of playing different classical and western instruments.

Description :
It is a solo performance.
Time Limit: 25 minutes

Halloween Night

A colorful amalgamation of music and dance, it’s where the beats take over the minds. It's the only event in the complete academic year in which girls and boys can mingle together. Famous DJ’s of north India like DJ JAM and DJ AKEEL have performed in the last few years.

Musical Night

The biggest event of the fest, where some of the best Bollywood celebrities and singers gather at Open Air Theatre. These celebrities entertain the audience with their mesmerizing performances.

Selfie le le re

It is an online Selfie contest.

Winners will be decided on the basis of likes that are common with the page and your picture.

Khoj Khajane Ki

Cryptic clues. Many paths. One treasure. The race is on.

Teams sabotaging the event will be disqualified immediately.
Requirements: The ability to run around is a must.
4 participants will form a team.
Coordinator’s decision is final and binding.

Gully Cricket

Play cricket with a little twist and a lot of fun! Make the gully your playground of cricket.

It will be a 5 over game.
Each team will have 5 Players.
Special rules will be there.

Face Painting

This event will provide great entertainment for both children and adults. One doesn’t have to be a professional artist to do face painting.

Bring a person whose face you will be painting.

Photo Scavenger Hunt

It is a fun, team-based scavenger hunt with an interesting twist where the goal is to bring back digital photos (or polaroids) of places and some interesting things.

2 person will form a team.
Time Limit : 1 hour.

Rangoli

The participant should prepare a Rangoli within the space provided.

Time Limit: 2 hours.
Use of stencils is not allowed.
Participants shall bring their own inventory.

Khana Khajana

It is a competitive cooking event.

There will be 2 rounds.
First round will be a written paper based on different recipies.
Participants have to arrange food items themselves and prepare a dish out of them.

Body Zorb

It is a unique group activity, where your upper body will be encased in our zorbs, while your legs are free to run from your opponents (the rest of the lunatics in zorbs trying to knock you over!)
